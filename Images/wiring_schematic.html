Kill-switch behavior

The MS-05 sits on the +V rail of the 11.1 V main trunk upstream of the ESC split. GND is a common return that bypasses the switch entirely. Throwing the switch cuts current to both ESCs, which also cuts the Left-ESC BEC that powers the Pi — every active subsystem (motors, Pi, receiver, IMU) drops in one action. Satisfies AWT 2.4 (full deactivation within 60 s via manual disconnect).
